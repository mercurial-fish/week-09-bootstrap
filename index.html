Self-Care

In this day and age of burnout and emotional exhaustion and overwhelm, self-care is more important than ever. However, a lot of people will only start taking care of themselves AFTER they are burnt-out. It's like if someone kept driving their car around until it was completely out of gas and then they are forced to walk to the gas station to fill up a container. It is so much extra work and effort and you're not really as full as you need to be. Good self-care is preventative and it needs to be addressed on all levels of who we are: Mind, body, and spirit. This site provides ideas and resources that will help you establish and maintain your self-care routine. All you have to do is click on the area you want to work on and follow the tab that interests you the most and we'll help you from there.
