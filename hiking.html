Hiking

Hiking is one of the most effective ways you can get your body moving and practice mindfulness. Nature overwhelms your senses and forces you stay present in the moment. And you can't get too lost in thought, or else you might stumble. The various inclines, the obstacles you have to get around, and uneven paths will get your heart pumping, as well as work muscles you probably haven't used in a while.
